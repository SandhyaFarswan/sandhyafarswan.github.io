Reducing Emission from Deforestation and Forest Degradation (REDD+)

Reducing Emission from Deforestation and Forest Degradation (REDD+) was proposed first in COP11 is a well crafted policy approach to help reduce deforestation in developing countries under the United Nations Framework Convention on Climate Change (UNFCCC). REDD+ contributes to greenhouse gas emission reductions, maintains or enhances forest biodiversity and ecosystem services.
